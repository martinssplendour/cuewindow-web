Privacy

Control what CueWindow listens to and keeps.

CueWindow is designed around explicit sessions, user-owned profiles, and clear data boundaries.

Audio processing

CueWindow listens only after a user starts a session. Audio is processed to create live transcripts and response suggestions.

Transcripts and responses

Session transcripts, generated responses, and conversation memory are stored only for signed-in users and are separated by account.

Files and profiles

Uploaded CVs, meeting documents, and extracted profile context are tied to the signed-in account and used to guide generated responses when relevant.

What is not allowed

CueWindow should not be used to fabricate credentials, impersonate another person, bypass platform rules, or misrepresent experience.

Account control

Authentication is handled through Supabase Auth. Password reset and account sessions are managed through secure token flows.

Questions

For privacy requests, account deletion, or data questions, contact support from the support page.
